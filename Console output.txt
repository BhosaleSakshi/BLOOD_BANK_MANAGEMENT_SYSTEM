   Basic Requirements for Donation 
	+ Donor must be in good health and feeling well
	+ Donor must be at least 18 years old and less than 65
	+ Donor must weigh at least 45 Kgs

		If Donor does not satisfy these requirements then Person is NOT eligible to Donate !

		If Donor satisfy these requirements then 
		Donar's blood sample is taken and tested 

			During TEST Hemoglobin and Blood groups are tested tested
				If Hemoglobin (HB) < 13 then Person can NOT Donate !
				If Hemoglobin (HB) >= 13 then Donar is eligible to Donate 

			Then Test Result are displayed

			According to Result Blood Packages are sorted and stored in Storage
